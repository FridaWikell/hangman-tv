    How to play

    The goal is to guess the secret word before the man is hung. In every guess, you have to guess at one letter. If the letter is in the correct word, the letter will appear at the blank lines. If it is wrong, one line will be added to the man. After six incorrect guesses, the man is hung and you lose the game...
